Video Info Tag (class CInfoTagVideo)
C++ » 7. Player

Detailed Description

Used for present video information

Class contains after request the on Kodi present information of played video or as entry from a list item.

It has the header #include <kodi/api3/player/InfoTagVideo.hpp> be included to enjoy it.
