Music Info Tag (class CInfoTagMusic)
C++ » 7. Player

Detailed Description

Used for present music information

Class contains after request the on Kodi present information of played music or as entry from a list item.

It has the header #include <kodi/api2/player/InfoTagMusic.hpp> be included to enjoy it.
